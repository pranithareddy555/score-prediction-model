Training the Model¶

1) Splitting the Data¶

# Defining X and y from the Data
X = data.iloc[:, :-1].values  
y = data.iloc[:, 1].values

# Spliting the Data in two
train_X, val_X, train_y, val_y = train_test_split(X, y, random_state = 0)
